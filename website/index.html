Key Features

The Mann Li Method combines advanced financial structuring with blockchain technology to create a robust bond issuance system.

Bond Series Support

Create multiple bond series with customizable parameters for different investor segments and market conditions.

Step-down Rate Model

Configurable interest rates that adjust over time, providing predictable returns for investors and manageable costs for issuers.

Maturity Claiming

Bondholders can claim maturity with bonus payment, incentivizing long-term holding and providing additional returns.

Automated Coupon Payments

Regular interest distributions to bondholders without manual intervention, ensuring timely and accurate payments.

Yield Optimization

Pluggable strategy system for maximizing returns on reinvested funds, enhancing overall yield for the ecosystem.

Risk Mitigation

Built-in contingency reserves protect against market volatility with multi-level emergency modes and threshold-based requirements.

Technical Architecture

The Mann Li Method creates an integrated financial ecosystem through three interdependent smart contracts working in concert.

MannLiBondToken

A specialized ERC20 token implementing a bond instrument with configurable parameters, maturity periods, and step-down rates.

MannLiReinvestment

Manages reinvestment strategies for bond yields with configurable rates, buyback mechanisms, and yield optimization.

MannLiContingencyReserve

Provides risk mitigation through a reserve fund with emergency modes, threshold-based requirements, and rate-limiting.
